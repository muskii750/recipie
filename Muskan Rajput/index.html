Ingredients Of Biryani

      basmati rice,
      chicken thighs, hung curd,
      onion, tomato,
      milk, saffron
      melange of whole and ground spices.

What are the steps to make biryani?

Put the boiling water in a saucepan,
add the washed rice, boil for 5 minutes and drain.
Layer the chicken mixture with the rice starting
with the rice then the chicken mixture
and repeat this twice. Garnish with cumin seeds,
and ginger. Lower the heat and simmer
for a further 10 minutes.
